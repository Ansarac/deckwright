# Charcoal Minimal  *(dark · understated · data-heavy)*

Near-monochrome charcoal with a single hot accent. Lets numbers and charts carry the slide.

| role | hex | role | hex |
|---|---|---|---|
| bg | `1A1A1A` | primary | `F2F2F2` |
| fg | `F2F2F2` | secondary | `2A2A2A` |
| muted | `9A9A9A` | accent | `E0A23B` |

- Fonts: **Arial Black / Arial** (or Consolas / Calibri)
- Motif: none — rely on a strict grid and size contrast
- Sandwich: fully dark
- Best for: data/financial reviews, understated technical decks

Shared rules apply (see `../themes.md`): one dominant color 60–70%, accent sparing, never a line under a title; colors are 6-digit hex without `#`; re-run the WCAG contrast self-check after any change.
