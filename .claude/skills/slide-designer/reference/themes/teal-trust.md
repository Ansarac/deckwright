# Teal Trust  *(dark · calm · reliability/cost/infra)*

Cohesive deep teal; reads as dependable and cost-conscious. Pairs well with icons in colored circles.

| role | hex | role | hex |
|---|---|---|---|
| bg | `0C1A1E` | primary | `02C39A` |
| fg | `F2F7F6` | secondary | `00A896` |
| muted | `8FA4A1` | accent | `028090` |

- Fonts: **Georgia / Calibri**
- Motif: thin left accent stripe (single-side border), repeated on every slide
- Sandwich: fully dark
- Best for: reliability/SRE, FinOps/cost, infrastructure

Shared rules apply (see `../themes.md`): one dominant color 60–70%, accent sparing, never a line under a title; colors are 6-digit hex without `#`; re-run the WCAG contrast self-check after any change.
