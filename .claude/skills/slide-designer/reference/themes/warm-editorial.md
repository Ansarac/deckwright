# Warm Editorial  *(light · humanist · narrative)*

Cream paper with terracotta — calm, literary, good for story-driven or exec narratives.

| role | hex | role | hex |
|---|---|---|---|
| bg | `FAF7F2` | primary | `B85042` |
| fg | `1E1B16` | secondary | `E7E8D1` |
| muted | `6B6358` | accent | `2C2A26` |

- Fonts: **Palatino / Garamond** (or Georgia / Calibri)
- Motif: minimal — generous margins do the work
- Sandwich: dark title + dark closing over light content
- Best for: narrative decks, brand/editorial, leadership comms

Shared rules apply (see `../themes.md`): one dominant color 60–70%, accent sparing, never a line under a title; colors are 6-digit hex without `#`; re-run the WCAG contrast self-check after any change.
