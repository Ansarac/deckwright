# Indigo Midnight  *(dark · premium · product/technical)*

The house look for product and engineering decks — deep near-black with a confident indigo and a warm amber accent.

| role | hex | role | hex |
|---|---|---|---|
| bg | `0E1116` | primary | `5B8DEF` |
| fg | `F5F7FA` | secondary | `1F2A44` |
| muted | `9AA3AF` | accent | `F2C14E` |

- Fonts: **Cambria / Calibri** (or Georgia / Calibri)
- Motif: `corner` (small stepped accent, top-right)
- Sandwich: fully dark, or dark title + dark closing
- Best for: product launches, technical talks, internal strategy

Shared rules apply (see `../themes.md`): one dominant color 60–70%, accent sparing, never a line under a title; colors are 6-digit hex without `#`; re-run the WCAG contrast self-check after any change.
